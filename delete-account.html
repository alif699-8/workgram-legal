Delete Your Workgram Account

You can permanently delete your Workgram account and all associated data directly within the app:

  1. Open the Workgram app and log in
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm the deletion

This immediately and permanently deletes your profile, bids, portfolio, follows, and verification documents. This action cannot be undone.

If you're unable to access the app, email support.workgram@gmail.com from your account's email address with the subject "Delete My Account" and we will process the deletion within 30 days.
